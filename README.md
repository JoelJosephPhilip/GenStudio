# GenStudio

GenStudio is an AI-powered product design platform built to help brands create studio-quality product visuals, marketing creatives, and rapid design variations.

Status: Under Development
This repository is currently a showcase for investors and early partners while the core platform is still being built.

## Vision

Enable every business to create premium product visuals in minutes using AI, without expensive studio shoots, complex editing pipelines, or large creative teams.

## Core Product Highlights

- AI scene generation for product backgrounds and lifestyle compositions
- Smart background removal for clean product isolation
- Region-based AI edits to refine specific parts of an image
- Layer-based design workflow for precise control
- Export-ready assets for ecommerce, ads, and social channels
- Usage and revision tracking for operational visibility

## AI Use Cases

- Ecommerce product listing images for Shopify, Amazon, and marketplace stores
- Performance marketing creatives for campaign A/B testing
- Seasonal catalog updates and rapid campaign refreshes
- Creative agency mockups and fast concept iteration
- SMB product photo enhancement without studio overhead
